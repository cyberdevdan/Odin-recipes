A BURGER

classic burger recipe typically starts with a juicy beef patty seasoned with salt and pepper, grilled or pan-fried to your desired doneness. The patty is then placed on a toasted burger bun. Layer it with toppings like lettuce, tomato slices, pickles, onions, and cheese (cheddar or American is common). Add condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise. For extra flavor, you can include bacon, avocado, or grilled mushrooms. Close the burger with the top bun, and serve it with fries or a side salad for a complete meal.

ingredients

  • Ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat) - 1 lb (for about 4 patties)
  • Burger buns - 4
  • Salt and pepper – to season the patties
  • Cheese slices (optional) – cheddar or American cheese
  • Lettuce leaves – 4 pieces
  • Tomato – sliced
  • Onion – sliced (red or white onion)
  • Pickles (optional) – sliced
  • Ketchup – for topping
  • Mustard – for topping
  • Mayonnaise – for topping
  • Butter – to toast the buns

preparation

  1. Prepare the Burger Patties:

    1. Take the ground beef and divide it into 4 equal portions.
    2. Gently shape each portion into a patty about ¾ inch thick.
    3. Season both sides of the patties generously with salt and pepper.

  2. Cook the Patties:

    1. Heat a grill or pan over medium-high heat. If using a pan, add a little oil to prevent sticking.
    2. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium doneness, or adjust the time based on your preferred level of doneness.
    3. If you're using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to let it melt.

  3. Toast the Buns:

    1. While the patties cook, lightly spread butter on the inside of the burger buns.
    2. Toast the buns on the grill or in a hot pan until they are golden brown, about 1-2 minutes.

  4. Prepare the Toppings:

    1. Wash and dry the lettuce leaves.
    2. Slice the tomato and onion thinly.
    3. Have the pickles and any additional toppings like avocado slices or bacon strips ready.

  5. Assemble the Burgers:

    1. Spread mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard on the inside of the toasted buns.
    2. Place the cooked patty on the bottom bun.
    3. Add a layer of lettuce, a slice of tomato, onion, and pickles (optional) on top of the patty.
    4. Add any other toppings like bacon, avocado, or grilled mushrooms.

  6. Serve:

    1. Place the top bun over the assembled burger.
    2. Serve with fries, chips, or a side of your choice. Enjoy!
